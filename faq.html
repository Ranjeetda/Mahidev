  • Will my home design reflect my personal style?

    Yes, we specialize in crafting luxurious, personalized designs for large-scale homes, bespoke villas, and high-value residential properties.

Ultimately, seeing my clients' joy and satisfaction when their dream space comes to life is priceless."

  • How do you balance functionality, aesthetics, and a client’s personal preferences in your designs?

    Balancing functionality, aesthetics, and client preferences involves collaboration and thoughtful design. I begin by understanding the client’s vision, ensuring the space meets practical needs while aligning with their style. Functionality is prioritized through ergonomic layouts and practical solutions, while aesthetics are enhanced using cohesive materials, colors, and lighting. Regular feedback ensures client preferences are respected, and I provide professional guidance when needed. By blending trends with timeless elements, I create designs that are functional, visually appealing, and personal.

  • What’s your educational background and how has it contributed to your journey as an interior designer?

    My journey as an interior designer is rooted in a lifelong passion for decorating and creating beautiful interiors. Even as a child, I found immense joy in rearranging spaces and imagining how they could be transformed.

Later, I pursued an MCA, further strengthening my problem-solving and project-management abilities. At age 34, I decided to pursue a professional course in interior design. This decision was the turning point that allowed me to combine my innate creativity with formal training.

    My diverse educational background and life experiences have given me a unique perspective, blending technical expertise with a deep understanding of aesthetics and functionality. This journey has been enriching , allowing me to channel my lifelong love for design into creating spaces that resonate with my clients.
